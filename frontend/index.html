Overview

The Movie Online Summit features an all-star line-up of widely respected industry professionals. Each topic features a conversation-style interview with our host, Arturs. Register for free and show up with an open-heart and an open-mind. Take notes if you want, or don’t. Watch the video from your computer, or listen while you’re on the go and keeping up with the kids. It costs nothing to attend and offers more value than we could even begin to assign a true dollar amount to.

Host

Arturs is arguably the most scandalous movie critic in the EU. He gained recognition with his review for the M.Night Shyamalan's "The Last Airbender" where he was among the handful who dared to publicly defend the movie and even compare it to big hits like "Die hard" and "Karate kid". Since then Arturs has been reviewing movies for the "Daily Sunshine" for 12 years now. He has published two books, is a regular guest on news shows and even hosts his own podcast on the latest news and rumors within movie industry.
